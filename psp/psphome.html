Questions About My Future College Plans With My Sister

Q: What is the first step in choosing a good college to aim for?

A: Based on what you are majoring, you can make a list of colleges.

Q: What do you have to do to get into a good college?

A: You have to have a good GPA, be top in your class, and be in extracurricular activities

Q: When is the right time to start preparing for college?

A: 9th grade is an ideal time to start preparing for college.

Q: How do you prepare for your SAT and ACT?

A: Join practice clubs or prep classes, and take a lot of practice tests.

Q: Is there anything I can do now to build a good foundation for college?

A: Build good vocabulary, improve your writing skills, and take advanced classes.

Q: As we wrap up this question session, I would like to ask one more question, is the process overwhelming?

A: It may seem overwhelming in the beginning but you and all your friends will make it there successfully.
