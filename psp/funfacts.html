Fun Goldendoodle Facts

Goldendoodles...

  • Are a cross between poodles and golden retrievers.
  • Come in multiple colors.
  • Are good starter dogs.
  • Are very cute.
  • Are generally hypoallergenic.
  • Are usually good with kids and other pets.
  • Are good service dogs.
